# Mechanical Design

The mechanical design of the **Green Node** focuses on durability, compactness, and ease of assembly. The enclosure is designed to house the core electronics, sensors, and power management systems while providing adequate exposure out to the environment.

## 3D CAD Enclosure

The render below illustrates the assembly of the primary tracking node, integrating our key sensors directly into the structural housing.

![Green Node CAD Assembly](/green-node-cad.jpeg)

### Mechanical Features
- **Integrated Environment Sensor (DHT11):** Protrudes gracefully from the side port to ensure the sensor captures ambient temperature and humidity seamlessly, free from any internal thermal interference.
- **Direct Soil Probing:** The soil moisture sensor is securely bottom-mounted, allowing the entire node to be easily pegged directly into the dirt.
- **Robust Casing:** The main body features heavy-duty styling with the "GREEN NODE" branding engraved cleanly on the surface.
